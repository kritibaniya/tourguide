Experience

There are a lot of things you might only be able to experience in Nepal. Here are few tips on how you can get great experience out of your trip.

Festivals

Hinduism is most followed religion in Nepal. It is a polytheistic religion, which means they believe in multiple gods. Therefore, there are plenty of festivals dedicated for different gods, year-round. Celebrating those festivals together with the locals is a very unique experience. You can see picture of people celebrating “holi”, which is festival of colors, on the left.

Jatra

Jatra means carnival in Nepali. These types of carnival are common among Newar community of Nepal. People are dressed in different types of traditional mascots while they play traditional instruments. There is no doubt that these carnivals are very lively.

Culture

Nepal is very rich in culture. With over one hundred different distinct ethnic group, there are lot of different culture and traditions. Which type of culture you get to know varies after which part of Nepal you are on. You can see an ethnic group called Sherpa from mountain areas wearing their traditional clothes in the picture.
