Places to visit

There are plenty of tourist attraction in the country. Every region is different from each other and have different things to offer. Here is a list of places you must visit and what you can expect on that place.

Kathmandu

Kathmandu is capital city of Nepal. Kathmandu is famous for temples. There are plenty of temples and many of them are over 1000 years old. The one you see in the picture is called Swayambunath temple. Syambhunath is also a great viewpoint since it lies in a hillock.

Pokhara

A lovely city 200km away from the capital city. Pokhara is one of the well-developed cities mixed with greenery and natural beauty. This might be the reason it is one of the most famous travel destinations among the tourists.

Bhaktapur

Bhaktapur is an ancient city of Nepal. Here lies Bhaktapur Durbar Square, which is an old palace. It is a perfect place to get to understand old architecture and temples. This place is also listed as a UNESCO heritage site.

Manang

Manang is a known for a great trekking place. It is a colder place with cold air and snow. This is not a typical city but a fun place to explore nature and trek. Hospitality of the locals and lovely views are the common things that attracts the tourists to Manang.

Lumbini

Lumbini is a holy place for Buddhist. Many people believe that it is birthplace of Buddha and visit Lumbini as a pilgrimage. The city is full of stupas and lovely nature. The calm environments in the stupas and temples helps people to be stress free and relax.
