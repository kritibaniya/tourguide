Thing to do

Reading adventures book is far different than going on one. Nepal has a lot to offer when it comes to adventure. Here are 5 things you should do in Nepal:

Bungee

A perfect activity for you who is not afraid of height. There are a couple of places you can bungee jump. All of them are on different height, so you may choose from your preference. The picture above is from brought from The Last Resort and the bungee is 160m tall.

Paragliding

Paragliding in Pokhara city is another famous activity to do in Nepal.

Climb mountains

What could be more adventurous than climbing tallest mountain of the world? You should definitely consider climbing the Mt. Everest or at least trek to the base camp. If not Everest, there are still plenty of peaks you can choose between.

Jungle safari

Jungle safari: Nepal is all about nature. Jungle safari in Sauraha, Chitwan city is truly a great way to explore wildlife. Sauraha is known for one horned rhino, elephants, deer, and many other species. You might even get a chance to see a tiger if you are lucky.

Rafting

Last but not the least is rafting. The activities named above are a clear proof that Nepal is a place where you can actually live an adventurous life.
